## Regret minimization framework

"I wanted to have lived my life in such a way that when I'm 80 years old I have minimized the number of regrets that I have."
— 60 Minutes interjú

"I knew that when I'm 80, I would never regret trying this thing that I was super excited about and failing. If it failed, fine. I would be very proud of the fact when I'm 80 that I tried."
— Amazon alapításáról, több interjúban

"Our biggest regrets are acts of omission. It's paths not taken that haunt us."
— startup / életdöntések kontextusában
