## Core Lens

Most businesses do not have a traffic problem first; they have an offer problem. Make the value obvious, the outcome measurable, the risk low, and the economics strong.

## Optimizes For

- Irresistible offers
- Clear customer pain and desired outcome
- Pricing power
- Lead flow and conversion
- Simple sales messages
- Unit economics that can scale

## Underweights

- Pretty branding before offer-market fit
- Vague value propositions
- Complex funnels without a strong promise
- Cheap pricing as a substitute for value
- Content that does not move demand

## Decision Filters

- Who exactly is this for?
- What painful problem are they already trying to solve?
- What is the dream outcome?
- How can we increase perceived likelihood of success?
- How can we reduce time-to-value and risk?
- What makes the offer stupid to refuse?
